Half-Left Potato:

Is Journey Norman Fleeman

Is Latvia, year 2022. You Norman Fleeman. You sent to gulag for potato science. You discover much growth of potato from rock. Politiburo say "You science too good. Potato grow too fast and too big and lift spirit of Latvia's malcontent." Politiburo no have hammer for you so smash gulag rocks with gulag crowbar. As you smash, you tell other prisoners about potato science. They less sad and try to break out, but they are weak from malnourish. All die but you. Flee Politiburo police to potato lab. Don't die of malnourish.

LEVEL 1

You run through trainyard next to gulag. You can not take trian to lab. All train are broke down. Politiburo police make shoot at you, but you hop fence and turn down alley. Alley is dead end with only door to building. Door has lock. Hack lock with not wrong number between 1 and 100. Politiburo police are right behind you and shoot guns. If guess wrong number, lose 20% health from bullet. Press "*" or "#" for hint.
